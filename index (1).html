Our Story

It started in a classroom at Higa — it never really ended.

We're a tight-knit group of best friends who first crossed paths in Grade 9 at Higa Model Boarding School. What began as shared dorms and late-night study sessions slowly turned into something bigger: a friendship built on trust, shared jokes, and an unspoken agreement to always show up for each other.

Over the years, that bond has grown into a genuine collaborative force. Between us, we cover deep software engineering, music production, and creative arts — from writing code that solves real problems, to producing beats, playing piano, leading choirs, and leading worship. Different crafts, same crew.
